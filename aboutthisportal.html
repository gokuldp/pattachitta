About this Portal

This is one of the eServices offered by the Government of Tamil Nadu for the Citizens to view the information related to their Lands, Houses located in Tamil Nadu.

Citizens should know or have in hand to elicit information from the website:

  • Survey Number and Sub Division Number or Patta Number or Owner Name
  • Application ID to know the status of Online Patta Transfer

The Citizens can view the following information:

View Patta/Chitta Copy & A-Register Extract - Rural

  • View Patta/Chitta
  • A-Register Extract
  • FMB Sketch
  • View Patta Order Copy
  • Application Status

View Patta/Chitta Copy & A-Register Extract - Urban

  • TSLR Extract
  • Application Status

Verify Web Issued Patta/Chitta Copy & A-Register Extract

  • Verify Patta / Chitta
  • Verify Government (Poramboke) / Private Land
